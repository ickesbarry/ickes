I am Head of the Department of Economics at the Pennsylvania State University. I am also Professor of Economics and Director of the Center for Research on International Financial and Energy Security, and a Founder of the The New Economic School in Moscow. Formerly, I was a Non-Resident Senior Fellow at the Brookings Institution. I am the past Chair of the Board of Directors of the National Council for Eurasian and East European Research. I was the President of the Association for Comparative Economic Studies during 2004.
